CHICKEN AND COCONUT RICE OMG

QUICK OVERVIEW

A delicious and nutritive plate based on two very common, cheap and easy to find ingredients: chicken and rice. A good introductory meal to the BASED COOKING method

INGREDIENT LIST

  • 250gr of chicken cut of choice
  • 250gr of rice of choice
  • A tablespoon of Butter
  • Anything you would like to add idk I'm not your daddy

STEPS

  1. Cook the chicken using the butter
  2. Add rice and water
  3. Let it finish cooking
  4. Enjoy like a motherfucking champ and get THOSE GAINZ
